An hour at Golden Crane is one of the kinder things you can give someone. Gift certificates are available for any service — relaxation massage, myofascial release, polarity therapy, or foot reflexology — and any session length.

### How it works

1. Email [aaronmmartz@gmail.com](mailto:aaronmmartz@gmail.com) with the service or dollar amount you'd like to give, and the recipient's name.
2. You'll receive a printable certificate by email, or a mailed paper certificate on request.
3. The recipient books their session at their convenience.

### Good to know

- Certificates do not expire.
- Transferable — your recipient can pass it along if they like.
- Available for studio sessions in Ann Arbor or mobile visits in the surrounding area.
